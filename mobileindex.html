St. Thomas Institute for Science and Technology proudly presents YUKTHI'25, the 2nd edition of its national-level tech fest. Building on the remarkable success of its inaugural event, YUKTHI has evolved into a prestigious platform that fosters innovation, collaboration, and the exchange of ideas among tech enthusiasts nationwide. With a vision to democratize education, this event champions free learning opportunities across various technology domains, breaking down financial barriers and empowering participants.
